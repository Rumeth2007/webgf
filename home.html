What Is ARS?

ARS IS THIS

An automated reporting system that creates reports for students based on their marks is a digital tool designed to simplify and streamline the process of generating academic reports. This system automatically compiles data on student performance, such as grades and test scores, and generates formatted reports such as report cards, performance summaries, and transcripts. By integrating with existing school data sources.
